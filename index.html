About Travel Hands


Travel Hands is a non profit startup launched on July 2016 that provides an on-line marketplace for travellers and local NGOs


Our Values


MISSION: TravelHands provides travellers and NGOs with a marketplace for short term social good activities and genuine experiences.


VISION: TravelHands helps people to do good while travelling and make a difference to local communities by spending a short part of their holidays working on local projects. Travellers become part of a community of peers. This community crowd-funds new NGO projects.
