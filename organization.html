Actions du coeur

'Actions du Cœur' are marauding held in Nice every Saturday evening. We are not an association, only a group of friends who wants to help people in difficulties. The purpose of these actions is to put a different perspective on the precarity . Indeed, we are all concerned in any way, because nobody is immune to find themselves in this situation.

HELP HOMELESS

Need:
Everyone is free to participate as they wish, within its means. We distribute an average of 80 meals per week that we all cook together in the kitchen of a restaurant. If you wish to participate you are welcome, any help is good to take! Our needs are varied : fruits, cakes, water bottles, shoes, hygiene products, clothing, ... but this is not an obligation , your time and your smile are already very valuable
